Maggi Recipe

This is a quick and tasty Maggi recipe that anyone can cook easily.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Maggi
  • 1 cup water
  • Masala sachet
  • Vegetables (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pan.
  2. Add noodles and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the masala.
  4. Cook and serve hot.
